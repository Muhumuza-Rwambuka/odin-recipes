Vaselopita - Greek New Years Cake

Description

Preparation time: 30 minutes
Cook time: 1 hour
Total time: 1 hour, 30 minutes
Servings: 12
Yield: 1 to 10 - inch round
pan

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter

    C)
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup blanched slivered almonds
